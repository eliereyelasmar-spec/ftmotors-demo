- **Scale:** 8,000+ dealers, 51 countries
- **Claim:** $10.1B in influenced sales
- **Strength:** Best-in-class chatbot, massive data
- **Weakness:** Enterprise pricing, franchise-focused
- **Pricing:** Not public, typically $2,000-$4,000+/month

#### Matador AI
- **Scale:** 1,000+ dealers, #1 on G2 rankings
- **Recent:** Nissan USA partnership (Nov 2025)
- **Strength:** Strong reviews, proven platform
- **Weakness:** Subscription regardless of results
- **Pricing:** Custom quotes, enterprise-level
- **For Fast Track:** Designed for franchises with corporate requirements

#### Numa
- **Scale:** 700+ dealers, $48M in funding
- **Model:** Pay-for-performance
- **Strength:** Aligned incentives, VC-backed
- **Weakness:** Pressure to upsell, may not prioritize small dealers
- **Pricing:** Performance-based, but complex
- **For Fast Track:** Interesting model but built for larger operations

#### Podium AI
- **Scale:** Broad market presence
- **Range:** $399-$3,500/month
- **Strength:** Easy setup, good for basic chat
- **Weakness:** Limited to top-of-funnel, not automotive-specific
- **For Fast Track:** Could work but lacks depth for auto dealership needs
